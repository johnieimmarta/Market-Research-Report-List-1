<p><strong>The Masonry Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type N</li><li>Type M</li><li>Type S</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Masonry cement is categorized into different types based on its properties and uses. Type N is a general-purpose cement suitable for above-grade masonry, offering moderate strength. Type M is high-strength cement ideal for heavy-load applications and below-grade structures. Type S is designed for structural walls and has higher strength than Type N but less than Type M, making it suitable for load-bearing masonry. Other types may include specialized mixes for specific applications, enhancing versatility in construction projects.</p></p>

<p><p>The air-defense missile system market comprises two primary types: Man-Portable Air-Defense Systems (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems. MANPADS are lightweight, shoulder-fired missiles designed for individual soldier use, offering mobility and rapid deployment for short-range threats. In contrast, Stationary/Platform-Mounted systems are integrated into larger platforms, such as vehicles or fixed installations, providing enhanced capabilities for longer-range targets and multi-layered defense. Both systems play crucial roles in safeguarding airspace from various aerial threats.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electromechanical Solenoid Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves</li><li>Sub-Step Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves</li><li>Pilot Solenoid Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical solenoid valves market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves** utilize the solenoid's electromagnetic force to open or close the valve directly, suitable for low-pressure applications. </p><p>2. **Sub-Step Direct-Acting Solenoid Valves** provide variable control over flow rates through incremental adjustments, enhancing precision in operation. </p><p>3. **Pilot Solenoid Valves** operate by controlling a secondary valve, allowing for larger flow capacities and higher pressures, making them ideal for complex systems. Each type serves distinct application needs in automation and control systems.</p></p>

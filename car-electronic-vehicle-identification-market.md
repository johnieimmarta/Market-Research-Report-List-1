<p><p>The Car Electronic Vehicle Identification market consists of two primary types: electronic tags and RFID readers. RFID readers are deployed at various checkpoints to scan the tags, facilitating seamless vehicle identification, monitoring, and access control. Together, these components enhance security, improve traffic management, and streamline processes in environments like toll booths and parking facilities.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Orthopedics Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedics Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) consist of various types, each serving specific surgical needs. Monopole Electrosurgical Units utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulating tissue, commonly used for precise incisions. Bipolar Electrosurgical Units employ two electrodes, allowing for localized tissue interaction, minimizing damage to surrounding areas. Vessel Sealing Units are designed to seal blood vessels through simultaneous cutting and coagulation, ensuring minimal bleeding during procedures. Each type enhances surgical efficiency, safety, and outcomes in orthopedic applications.</p></p>
